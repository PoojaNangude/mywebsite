Umaid Bhavan

Umaid Bhavan Palace

Oh yes!!!The famous Umaid Bhavan in Jodhpur; where Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas got married. It is on of the world's largest private residence. A part of the palace is managed by Taj Hotels. Named after Maharaja Umaid Singh, grandfather of the present owner Gaj Singh. The palace has 347 rooms and is the principal residence of the former Jodhpur royal family. A part of the palace is a museum.

Lake Pichola

Lake Pichola

Lake Pichola, situated in Udaipur city in the state of Rajasthan, is an artificial fresh water lake on which the famous Lake Palace has been built. It is one of the several contiguous lakes, and developed over the last few centuries in and around the famous Udaipur city.
